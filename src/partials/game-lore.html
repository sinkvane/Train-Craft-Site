GAME LORE

The game takes place in the distant future after an unknown disaster that drove the survivors into the depths of the Earth. Though the Union of Free Cities provides for the settlements, fierce competition rules, as those who extract the most resources get the best equipment.

One day, when exploring the mines, the player tumbles into an abandoned hangar to find a highly advanced relic digger train that had been stored for many centuries. And now this machine is yours to command.
