game features
  • Configuration - players can configure their Digger Train’s layout of wagons and engines in the manner that suits them best, but it will affect mission outcomes and yield.
  • Upgrades allow players to constantly improve their Digger Train’s components.
  • Resources collection - players need to pummel their way through crumbling labyrinths while facing a slew of dangers.
  • Hazards lie in wait around every corner of the maze of underground labyrinths players venture through, as monsters can quickly reduce the player’s train characterists.

GAME CONCEPT

When at the helm of the Digger Train, players pummel their way through dangerous mines, collect valuable resources to upgrade their machine, and fight off the monsters that inhabit the depths!
